Overview

The Criminal podcast has expanded into book publishing, launching its first series featuring The Paramedics, Lavender Scare, and The Palace of Justice. This project includes the design of these three books and their landing page.

Challenge/Problem

How can a podcast brand expand its visual identity into a new medium? Criminal has been a leading true-crime podcast for a long time, with a strong foundation to broaden its reach. Now they are entering the publishing world—an entirely different landscape. The challenge was to ensure that the books and the podcast felt cohesive under the same brand identity.

Solution

The first series—The Paramedics, Lavender Scare, and The Palace of Justice—draws from the podcast’s most compelling stories. My role was to design the book covers and the landing page for this new publishing venture. I ensured that the overall graphic language remained faithful to the Criminal brand while introducing new design elements that reflect their expansion into print media.

Challenge—Podcast to Books

Although the following, “The Paramedics,” “Lavender Scare,” and “The Palace of Justice,” are all independent episodes in the podcast channel, they represent resilience and a strong will to fight and build the greater good. The challenge was how to interpret each podcast episode into a different media and how to make them look different but not too different from each other.

Challenge—Designing With Code

The biggest challenge was to make sure the promotional platform for this new service was using the same visual language and assets but should look different at the same time.

With that in mind, I drafted the UI design in Figma and finished it while coding from scratch.
