Hanna Shibata is an Art Director and a Graphic Designer.

Excelling in solving problems through communication strategies and creating solid brand narratives.

Hanna Shibata is an art director/graphic designer with 10+ years of experience across continents and various fields.

After starting her career as a packaging designer in Tokyo, Hanna expanded her role into branding design as an in-house creative. There, she forged and managed relations with internal and external stakeholders, building a creative team from scratch. Eventually, taking the leap to pursue her graduate studies at Pratt Institute, Hanna left Tokyo and has continued her career as an in-house Art Director in the United States.

She is now taking a time-off to expand her skill set and explore the realm of digital design freely.
